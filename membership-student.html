Student Membership

Welcome to the Student Membership section. Here you can find information about joining our community and the benefits of membership.

Student Membership with the International Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF) is available to individuals currently enrolled in a fire science, emergency services, or related public safety program at an accredited institution.

This membership category is designed to support the next generation of fire service professionals by providing access to valuable resources, mentorship opportunities, and networking with active fire service personnel across the globe.

Student members are encouraged to actively participate in ICOFF initiatives, attend conferences and training events, and engage with their local ICOFF chapter. While student members do not have voting rights, their voices and contributions are valued and essential to the future of the profession.

Benefits of Student Membership:

  • Access to exclusive training and educational resources
  • Discounted registration for ICOFF events and conferences
  • Mentorship opportunities with experienced fire service professionals
  • Networking with local, national, and international members
  • Opportunities to contribute to ICOFF publications and outreach initiatives

To apply for student membership, please click the Student Membership Application link below. Complete the application and submit proof of current enrollment in a qualifying program.

ICOFF is committed to helping you build a strong foundation for a successful career in the fire service. Join today and take your first step toward becoming a leader in the field.
