Apply for Student Membership and join the next generation of fire and emergency service professionals with ICOFF.

Effective Date: 01/2025

Student Membership Application

Become part of the International Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF) student community. Student Membership is open to individuals currently enrolled in fire science, emergency services, or related public safety programs at accredited institutions.

Why Register as a Student Member?

  • Access exclusive training and educational resources
  • Discounted registration for ICOFF events and conferences
  • Mentorship from experienced fire service professionals
  • Networking with local, national, and international members
  • Opportunities to contribute to ICOFF publications and outreach

Student members are encouraged to participate in ICOFF initiatives, attend events, and engage with their local chapter. While student members do not have voting rights, your voice and contributions are valued and essential to the future of the profession.

To apply, please complete the registration form below and submit proof of current enrollment in a qualifying program.

Have questions? Contact us for more information about student membership.
