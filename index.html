Welcome to the, Council of Fire FIghters

The International Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF), headquartered in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, is a global leader dedicated to advancing firefighting as a profession through education, collaboration, and innovation. For over two decades, we’ve championed the interests of firefighters worldwide, setting rigorous industry standards and delivering cutting-edge certifications and training programs.

Our Mission

Advance the profession of fire fighting: Promote the interests of fire fighters worldwide and support their professional development.

Our Vision

ICOFF fosters a global network of professionals committed to excellence in fire safety, emergency management, and disaster resilience. Together, we build a safer tomorrow.

Our Values

- Respect for self, others, and the environment.
- Integrity in actions and academics.
- Collaboration across differences.

ICOFF Certifications

At ICOFF, we offer globally recognized certifications designed to elevate firefighting careers at every level. Whether you’re starting as a Certified Fire Fighter (CFF), advancing into leadership with the Certified Fire Officer (CFO), training future responders as a Certified Fire Instructor (CFI), or coordinating large-scale crises as a Certified Emergency Manager (CEM), our programs blend rigorous theory with real-world application.
